Our Story

Crafting Spaces That Speak

Stasha Interior was born from a singular belief — that every room holds the potential to become a masterpiece. For over a decade, we have transformed homes across the country into spaces of rare distinction and enduring beauty.

Our approach marries the precision of architectural thinking with the intuition of fine art, resulting in interiors that are not merely beautiful, but deeply and uniquely yours.
